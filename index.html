About

My name is Sebastien Correaud, Web Developer in Montpellier (France). I joined the CGI's Webfactory Team (formerly Logica) for analysis and development tasks within various web projects using PHP frameworks (eZ Publish, Drupal, Symfony). Subsequently I have contributed to the birth and evolution of a mobile development pole (iOS, HTML5 Cross-Platform, Android). I also contributed to the evolution of the Webfactory team by achieving various missions like training junior staff, technical audits and technology watch. Nowadays I am a Fullstack Web Developer for 1001pharmacies. I'm specialized in Javascript (Node, React, ES6/ES7, Webpack, Babel). I'm also an active OSS contributor and project maintainer.
